## 3. Solution - Walrus Haulout
**Data marketplace with legal protection**

**4 Core Features:**
1. **Walrus Storage** - Secure storage that can't be deleted or modified
2. **Sui Blockchain** - Records every transaction, can't be faked
3. **Nautilus TEE** - Security chip that creates authentication certificates
4. **Copyright Protection** - Clear purchase history that works in court

## 4. Live Demo

### Step 1: Upload Dataset
1. Select CSV file (e.g., iot-sensors.csv)
2. Fill in details: name, description, price, access type
3. Click "Create Dataset"
4. **Show:** Storage ID + Certificate on blockchain

### Step 2: Download with Proof
1. Download dataset from "My Datasets"
2. **Show:** Green checkmark from Nautilus verification
3. **Show:** Complete transaction history:
   - Who uploaded, when
   - Certificate number
   - Who purchased, when
   - Everything has timestamp and fingerprint

### Legal Protection
**Emphasize:** Every action is recorded on blockchain
- Who sold (seller's address)
- When sold (exact time)
- What was sold (data fingerprint)
- Who bought (buyer's address)
- When bought (transaction record)

**Result:** Legal proof that courts accept
